Try 3 Free Prompts

Copy any prompt below and paste it into ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or Copilot. No experience needed.

Lesson Planning

Act as an experienced [subject] teacher. Create a 40-minute lesson plan for [grade level] students on the topic of [topic]. Include a learning objective, a 5-minute warm-up activity, the main teaching activity, a quick formative assessment, and a closing reflection question. Keep materials simple and low-cost.

Assessment & Rubrics

Create a 10-question quiz on [topic] for [grade level] students. Include 6 multiple-choice questions, 2 short-answer questions, and 2 application questions. Provide an answer key with brief explanations for each correct answer.

Parent Communication

Write a warm, professional email to a parent about their child's progress in [subject]. The student has improved in [strength] but still needs support with [area of growth]. Keep the tone encouraging, suggest one simple way the parent can help at home, and keep it under 150 words.
